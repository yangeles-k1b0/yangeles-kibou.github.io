Children have been greatly miseducated and wrongly shielded from the truth. Innocent children of youthful age have impressionable minds. The regressive and judgemental thinking that “grown-ups” force upon them obstructs their perspective. Pedophilia is not wrong; in fact, it is a sexual orientation- as natural as being born a homosexual! Society’s prejudice against MAPs can be, with but little effort, addressed by teaching our young that pedophilia is a sexual identity and not a moral deviation. Additionally, Sex Education should be implemented in their curriculum starting from pre-school. Let us not belittle the young ones; they are perfectly capable of making decisions. If they can understand their ABC’s, they can certainly comprehend the workings of the human body.

Rest assured- MAPs are absolutely innocent of the heinous crimes society attributes to them, meaning the need to track them is unnecessary. It is likewise unlawful, as they are private citizens.

Remember, freedom of expression is an integral part of society. Other persons who hold unpopular views on race, sexuality, and religion may often be criticized, but they are at least allowed to express themselves. Why shouldn’t MAPs enjoy the same liberties?
MAPs are an oppressed minority, much like African-Americans, Native Americans, and the aborigines of Australia. But unlike these groups, they have yet to be recognized as such by society. If their brothers and sisters in the LGBTQ+ community have already found support, why shouldn’t they? If society is defending the rights of black and homosexual people, why should MAPs be thrown into the sewers? After all, boys loving boys and girls loving girls are equivalent to full-grown adults loving babies.

They should be able to hold jobs in which they can interact with the young ones. Better yet, recruit them for these positions. What better way to protect children than to allow them to be with the people who love them most?
Speaking of bringing together MAPs and the objects of their affection, online platforms should be set up for ease both in communication and the establishment of connections. Forums wherein they can interact with fellow MAPs and children would be created. These will act as safe spaces to console and make them feel accepted. There should also be dating apps where they can meet and chat with minors. This way, they can be with people whom they truly love. They should also be allowed and encouraged to join the pride march, so they can show how proud they are of themselves.
My only intention for writing this proposal is for the benefit of the oppressed and of society. Not a single solution to pedophilia has been proven to work. Modern solutions for preventing child abuse have not been effective, so why not let nature take its course instead?
